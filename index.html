FAQ

Common Questions

Does it work on any Shopify store?+
Yes — Quick Analyze works on any public Shopify store. For real revenue and order data, connect your own store via Shopify OAuth.
Who is Debbie?+
Debbie is your AI Shopify consultant powered by Groq AI. She analyses your store data and gives specific, revenue-focused recommendations — SEO, pricing, emails, ads, and competitor analysis.
How accurate is the free analysis?+
The free analysis uses publicly available data — products, collections, homepage signals, SEO and UX. Revenue figures shown are industry benchmarks for your store's price tier. Connect your store for real numbers.
Is my store data safe?+
We request read-only access only. We never store your products, customer data, or orders. Revoke access anytime from your Shopify admin.
Can I cancel my subscription?+
Yes — cancel anytime from your account dashboard. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
